National Word Hunt Festival

Where the Nation's Top Young Talent Cultivates Their Skills in the Popular GamePigeon App

About the Festival

The National Word Hunt Festival is the premier event for aspiring champions of the popular GamePigeon Word Hunt game. Held annually, this festival brings together the nation's top young talent to enhance their skills through a variety of activities.

Activities

  • Private Coachings
  • Workshops
  • Master Classes
  • Public Performances of Skill

How to Apply

Applications are open to all talented Word Hunt players aged 13-21. To apply, please submit a video of your gameplay, a personal statement, and two letters of recommendation.
